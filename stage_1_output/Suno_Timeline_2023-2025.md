# Suno Timeline: 2023–2025

A chronological history of Suno's product launches, partnerships, legal battles, and community milestones. This timelines is a result of synthesis from news articles archieve mentioning Suno AI and historical messages on announcement and updates sub-room on Suno official community Discord chat server ([text](https://discord.com/servers/suno-1069381916492562582))
